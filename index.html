Daan de Geus

I'm an assistant professor in the Mobile Perception Systems Lab at the Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), in the Netherlands. I did my PhD at TU/e, and was a visiting PostDoc in the Computer Vision Lab at RWTH Aachen University, headed by Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe. My research focuses on machine learning for visual and multiomodal scene understanding.
